A Little About Myself

Aside from being a Bachelor of Science in Visual Communication Design student, I bring with me over three years of practical experience gained through various freelance and job opportunities, as well as my own radio station’s website and app building and maintenance. Through these opportunities, I honed and practiced not only my technical skills, but also my interpersonal, communication, teamwork, and organizational skills, preparing me for this moment. A little over a year ago, I decided to establish the HydeFM radio station, with a desire to bring people together through music. Doing this, I was able to combine the two things I love the most – technology and people. I utilized WordPress to build a functional website where you can listen, watch, browse shows and content, and interact with others. I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front-end content, and PHP for back-end editing. I’m maintaining the website daily, from content creation and review, through error checks and fixes, design, and functionality updates, and more.
